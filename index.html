About Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Dr. Muhammad Yunus is a Bangladeshi social entrepreneur, economist, and Nobel Peace Prize winner. He is best known for his pioneering work in microfinance, particularly through the establishment of Grameen Bank, which provides small loans to those in need, enabling them to start or expand small businesses and break the cycle of poverty.

Dr. Yunus’s work has inspired a global movement in social business and financial inclusion. His dedication has led to a lasting impact on countless communities worldwide. Beyond Grameen Bank, Dr. Yunus has initiated numerous projects aimed at addressing poverty, healthcare, and education.

Educational Qualifications

  • Bachelor of Arts in Economics - Dhaka University, Bangladesh
    Graduated with honors, Dr. Yunus showed an early interest in economics and social sciences.
  • Fulbright Scholarship - Awarded a Fulbright scholarship to study in the United States, a prestigious honor for international students.
  • Ph.D. in Economics - Vanderbilt University, USA
    Specialized in economic development, completing a dissertation on rural economic systems.
  • Honorary Degrees - Received more than 50 honorary degrees from renowned universities worldwide in recognition of his contributions to social entrepreneurship, including:
    • Harvard University
    • University of Oxford
    • University of Tokyo
    • Yale University

Achievements

  • 2006 - Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize for his work in microfinance and founding Grameen Bank
  • 2009 - Received the Presidential Medal of Freedom from the United States
  • 2010 - Honored with the Congressional Gold Medal for his work in social entrepreneurship
  • 2011 - Founded Grameen America to expand microfinancing opportunities in the U.S.
  • 2012 - Launched Grameen Healthcare to provide affordable health solutions
  • 2015 - Established Yunus Social Business to help expand social businesses globally

Notable Projects by Dr. Yunus

Grameen Bank: The revolutionary microfinance institution.

Grameen Healthcare: Providing affordable healthcare solutions.

Grameen Education: Ensuring quality education for the underprivileged.
